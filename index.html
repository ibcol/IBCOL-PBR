Mission

Practical Blockchain Review (PBR)

Practical Blockchain Review (PBR) is an archive intended for an audience of esoteric knowledge seekers, such as those from academic institutions, researchers, and niche practitioners. PBR is an initiative of the International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL).
